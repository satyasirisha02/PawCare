🚨 EMERGENCY FIRST AID

Stay Calm. Act Rapidly. Save Lives.

When unexpected emergencies happen, every second counts. Bookmark this protocol to maintain composure and respond safely.

  • 🔒 Secure the Area: Keep yourself and your pet safe from immediate hazards (traffic, power lines, fire).
  • 📞 Emergency Vet Contact: Instantly notify your emergency vet clinic to prepare their trauma bay.
  • 🩹 Basic Stabilization: Carefully apply pressure to bleeding zones with sterile materials and restrict rapid movement.

Rapid Checklist

Pack this essential go-bag for immediate travel readiness:

  • ✓ Primary Vet & Poison Control Hotline numbers.
  • ✓ Sterile adhesive bandages, surgical gauze, and splints.
  • ✓ A secure, well-ventilated carrier and safety harness.
Hotline Support: 1-800-PAW-HELP
